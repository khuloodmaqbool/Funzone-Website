About
Funzone Park

Funzone Park is a premier destination for families and thrill-seekers alike. Nestled in the heart of Playtown, it offers a wide array of attractions and entertainment options for all ages and interests. From exhilarating rides to family-friendly activities, there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Regulation

We value your feedback and inquiries. Whether you have questions, suggestions, or just want to say hello, please get in touch. Your communication is important to us.

Height and Age Restriction

Check signage or ask park staff about height and age restrictions for certain rides and attractions.

Food and Beverage Policy

All food and beverages are inspected upon entry. Outside alcohol and glass containers are prohibited.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is allowed only in designated areas. Please refrain from smoking elsewhere to maintain a comfortable environment for all visitors.

Park Cleanliness

Please help keep the park clean by using designated trash receptacles. Your cooperation ensures a pleasant experience for everyone.
